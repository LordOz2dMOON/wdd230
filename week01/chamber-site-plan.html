Lagos Chamber of Commerce

This site name was chosen because the website caters to businesses in Lagos.

Site Purpose

  • To serve as a regional economic catalyst, bringing together local businesses and resources to drive economic growth.
  • To provide essential information and resources for businesses and individuals within the community

Scenarios

  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors
  • What has been the recent population growth in the area and how does it affect local businesses
  • what events and intitiatives does the chamber have planned for this month in terms of business networking

Color Schema

The color schema for this website will consist of the following colors

  • Primary Color:[Princeton Orange], used for headings and accents
  • Secondary Color:[Davy's gray], used for paragraph text and background colors

Typography

The following fonts will be used for this website:

  • Primary Font[Playfair Display], used for headings
  • Secondary Font[Roboto], used for body text and other text elements
